registering a right owner
register > right owners



duplicate check registration of right owners are carried out in two steps. In a first step, a set of mandatory information is asked to the operator. Once the operator clicks on "new", the solution checks if similar right owners have been registered before. This duplicate check displays the potential duplicates found in the local database, should some exist. The operator can cancel the registration or continue.
main information the second step is the completion of the remaining information. In the "main" tab, an operator can manage the party general information, including gender, country of birth, etc. Names, identifiers and affiliation (or assignments) can be also completed and updated. The information of the main tab are subject to the synchronization process.
local information in the "local" tab, all additional information can be entered such as addresses, contacts, bank account, documents as well as any specific fields needed by the CMO. The information of the local tab are never disseminated, even when the right owner is synchronized
